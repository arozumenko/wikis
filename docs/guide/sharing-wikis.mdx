---
title: "Sharing Wikis"
description: "Control who can see your wikis — keep them private or share with your whole team."
---

Every wiki has one of two visibility states:

| State | Icon | Who can see it |
|-------|------|---------------|
| **Personal** | 🔒 | Only you (the owner) |
| **Shared** | 🌐 | All authenticated users on the instance |

<Note>
  There is no per-user or per-team sharing — a wiki is either private to you or visible to everyone on the instance. Role-based access control is on the roadmap.
</Note>

## Sharing from the dashboard

Hover over any wiki card you own. In the bottom-right, you will see two action icons:

- **Share icon** (⇶) — appears when the wiki is Personal; click to make it Shared
- **Lock icon** (🔒) — appears when the wiki is Shared; click to make it Personal

The badge on the card updates immediately.

## Sharing from the wiki viewer

Click the **↗ Share link** button in the top-right corner of any page to copy a direct deep-link to that page. Recipients must be logged in to view it — if they aren't, they will be redirected to the login page first.

## Ownership and permissions

| Action | Owner | Other authenticated user |
|--------|-------|--------------------------|
| View (Shared wiki) | ✅ | ✅ |
| View (Personal wiki) | ✅ | ❌ |
| Edit visibility | ✅ | ❌ |
| Refresh / re-generate | ✅ | ❌ |
| Delete | ✅ | ❌ |
| Ask questions | ✅ | ✅ (on shared wikis) |

## Deleting a wiki

1. On the dashboard, hover the wiki card and click the **trash** (🗑) icon
2. A confirmation dialog asks you to confirm — deletion is permanent
3. All pages and indexed artifacts are removed from storage

<Warning>
  Deletion cannot be undone. If you want to regenerate the same repository later, you will need to re-run generation from scratch.
</Warning>
